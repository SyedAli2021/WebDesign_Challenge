Latitude vs. Humidity

There is no strong correlation detected between latitude and humidity. There is a strong concentration of cities with a 100% humidity that spanned from latitude -50 to latitude 80. While there were some cities with humidity of 50% or less, the bulk was about 60% with the most at 100%.
