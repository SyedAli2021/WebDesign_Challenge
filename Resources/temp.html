Latitude vs. Maximum Temperature

As expected, there is a strong correlation between the maximum temperature and latitude. The latitudes range from -60 to 80 for this dataset which spans below south america and up to the north pole. So the higher latitudes were facing extream cold temperates and the lower altitudes were facing more moderate temparatures. Around the equator, the temperate was around 75 degrees. At the north pole, the temperature was below -50 degrees Fahrenheit.
